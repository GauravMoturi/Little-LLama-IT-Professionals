About Us


Little Llama IT Professionals is a website that was developed to help other people that didn't know anything about any IT courses, learn new things about those couses and to teach people the basic and advanced functions of the supported courses. Little Llama IT Professionals wants to see you improve and develop and to support you and make sure you get all the help you need we have our support staff online so you always have the option to ask them questions. We also teach you a course in 10 group sessions, the group sessions are made up of you, other members and users, and one of our teaching staff, if you have a question in the session you can always ask your session teacher and tehy will be happy to help. Best of all you can learn and be part of a session from anywhere, at home, in the library, or in the subway and you don't even need to be here physically.
